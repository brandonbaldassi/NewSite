<h3 style="margin: 0;">Design • Engineering • Fabrication • Competition</h3>

The world needs passionate, curious people who are excited to innovate and create meaningful change. Motorsports allows me to express these qualities with a science-based approach to designing, building, testing and racing cool cars. I share this journey to bridge motorsports with STEM and inspire others to tackle challenges with curiosity, creativity, and resilience. 

Whether you're a fellow enthusiast, an aspiring builder, or a brand looking for authentic validation and impact, I believe in building a community where practical skills fuel personal growth and open doors to possibilities on and beyond the racetrack.
