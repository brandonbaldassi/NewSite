Research

My research interests in physics of medical imaging focus on development of novel positron emission tomography (PET) for current and emerging clinical applications, solid-state imaging sensors, reconstruction algorithms, and novel radiotracers. I am proud of my involvement in the development and commercialization of the Radialis PET Imager, offering best-in-class performance and sees clinical use for a variety of indications, from breast cancer to Alzheimer's. I have also accrued expertise in achieving and maintaining regulatory compliance (FDA, Health Canada) and quality management systems (ISO 13485:2016, MDSAP).
